# Alfred's Holiday

*A night-glide through deep space, painted in ink.*

A relaxing single-file browser game. You are Alfred, a wisp of light drifting through
ten sumi-e-painted regions of space — gathering light, riding real gravity around
planets, and playing an ever-changing koto solo with your own flight path.

**Play:** open `index.html` in any modern browser (or enable GitHub Pages for this repo
and play at the Pages URL). Works on desktop and mobile. No dependencies, no build,
no assets — all art is drawn on canvas and all music is synthesized live.

**Art bible:** `bestiary.html` — live-animated mockups of every creature and every
level's sky-body.

## Controls

| Input | Action |
|---|---|
| `↑` / `W` / `Space` (or on-screen ↑) | thrust up |
| `↓` / `S` (or on-screen ↓) | thrust down |
| `←` / `A` — yes, left | surge faster |
| `→` / `D` | ease off |
| `i` | toggle ink & paper / night mode |
| `m` | sound on/off |

Planets bend your path with real inverse-square gravity. Never touch the surface.
Your ember fades — gather the orange light to burn on. Progress autosaves at each
new region (localStorage).

## The ten levels

1. **The Amber Drift** — cranes, the vermilion sun
2. **The Glass Deep** — koi and water lilies, a silver moon
3. **The Rose Veil** — cherry blossoms, the bloom moon *(music turns minor)*
4. **The Violet Reach** — rising lanterns, reverse gravity, the high lantern
5. **The Sea of Pandas** — pandas adrift on bamboo, the bamboo sun
6. **Minx's Cavern** — cats, the cat-eye crescent
7. **The Wandering Peaks** — goats patrolling floating mountains, the cold sun
8. **The Stone River** — an asteroid belt, sky whales, narwhals and manatees, the eclipse
9. **Rainbow Paradise** — everything, in colour, under the ringed sun
10. **The Visitors** — jellies, squid, watchers and saucers beneath twin green moons
